Professional profile

A teacher who makes English usable.

Mine brings warmth, structure, and real communication into the classroom.

Her teaching journey began in 2016 as a volunteer English teacher with The Educational Volunteers Foundation of Türkiye. Since then, she has taught in bilingual kindergarten and public primary school settings, led a Montessori-inspired English-speaking workshop, and supported other teachers with lesson planning and classroom practice.

She plans around learners' levels and goals, using games, movement, drama, songs, storytelling, and interactive activities to make English meaningful and memorable. She also prepares personalised learning plans to support each learner's progress.

A strong fit for

International schools, Thai private schools, language institutes, and primary programmes looking for a teacher who can connect with children and collaborate with a team.
